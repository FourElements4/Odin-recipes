Soup Dumplings Recipe

Description

This is the famous xiaolongbao (or) soup dumplings. Please enjoy making this dish.

Ingredients

  • Ground pork
  • Pork skin
  • Pork neck bones
  • Water
  • Slices ginger
  • Scallion
  • shaoxing wine
  • Dumpling dough

Steps

  1. Add the pork skin and pork bones and cover with cold water.
  2. Rinse off the bones and the skin after boiling.
  3. Put everything back in along with 950ml of water, ginger, scallion and wine.
  4. Bring the pot to a boil and then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 2 hours.
  5. Turn off the heat, allow the soup to cool, and strain the liquid into a bowl.
  6. Once the liquid is completely cooled, cover and refrigerate overnight.
  7. Make the filling.
  8. Mix the filling with meat gelatin(which you obtained after cooling).
  9. Pack with the dough and steam it in the bamboo steamer basket over high heat for 8 minutes.
  10. Prepare soy sauce for dipping.
